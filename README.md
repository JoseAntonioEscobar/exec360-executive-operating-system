# EXEC360 – Executive Operating System

EXEC360 is a portfolio project designed to demonstrate executive operations management, accountability systems, KPI visibility, risk monitoring, strategic planning, and decision-support capabilities.

The platform simulates how a COO, Integrator, Chief of Staff, Director of Operations, or Founder can manage an organization through a centralized Executive Operating System.

---

## Overview

The objective of EXEC360 is not simply to display metrics.

The platform is designed to answer a more important question:

**"What should leadership focus on today?"**

EXEC360 combines executive reporting, operational visibility, accountability tracking, risk management, and strategic execution into a single decision-support environment.

---

## Key Features

### Executive Briefing

AI-style executive summary highlighting priorities, risks, and recommended focus areas.

### Business Health Score

Consolidated operational, financial, execution, compliance, customer, and people metrics.

### Priorities Center

Executive-level visibility into critical initiatives, ownership, due dates, and business impact.

### Decision Center

Action-oriented recommendations designed to support leadership decision-making.

### Execution Command Center

Project tracking, milestone monitoring, and execution oversight.

### Accountability Tracker

EOS-inspired ownership, commitments, due dates, and status monitoring.

### Risk Radar

Operational, financial, compliance, supplier, and execution risk visibility.

### Financial Oversight

Executive-level reporting of cash position, profitability, margins, and financial health.

### Strategic Initiatives

Tracking of 90-day priorities, annual goals, and transformation initiatives.

### Meeting Command Center

Level-10 inspired operational cadence, action items, decisions, and follow-ups.

### Founder View

Focused view of what requires leadership attention, delegation, or monitoring.
